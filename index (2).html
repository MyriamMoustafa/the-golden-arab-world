THE TWO MOST FAMOUSE FOODS IN UAE:


1- Harees :

Harees is one of the famous popular dishes in the United Arab Emirates .
this dish is often eaten at family occasions, especially wedding parties and national and religious holidays, especially during the holy month of Ramadan. can be prepared with chicken or meat.

(Ingredients)


Crushed wheat soaked for two hours.
A large onion, cut into cubes.
One kilo of boneless red meat, cut into medium-sized cubes.
Ground cumin.
Half a cup of olive oil.
A pinch of salt.
4 liters of hot water.

2- Jasheed

Made from baby sharks, “jasheed” is the most famous Emirati dish The “jasheed” dish is considered one of the most famous meals in Emirati cuisine, as it provides the body with many beneficial nutrients that give it great energy and vitality. Jesheed is a fairly expensive traditional seafood dish, especially since it consists of baby sharks known as “boy’s boy.” A group of Emirati spices and seasonings known as “Al-Bazzar” are added to it, and it is cooked in several specific steps.

(Ingredients)


Walid fish (alrayur)
Two medium onions, cut into slices
Mashed garlic clove
