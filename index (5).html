1- kabsa:



(The historical story)


The Bukharian Kabsa in its current form originated in the sixties and seventies of the last century in the Al-Sharqiya neighborhood in the city of Taif and in a neighborhood called Al-Bukhariyya, a neighborhood inhabited by the Bukharian Muslim community fleeing Uzbekistan and its surroundings during the communist era, after the Bukharians took refuge in Saudi Arabia to escape their religion and were immediately allowed to reside.The first people to invent kabsa were the poor Spaniards, as they used to collect the leftover food of the nobles, combine it, and mix it in one dish called bahiya, which is a distorted word from the word bakiyya.
