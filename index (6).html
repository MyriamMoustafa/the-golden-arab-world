THE TWO MOST FAMOUSE FOODS IN LEBANON:


1- Tabouli



Tabouli is one of the types of salads that is famous in the Levant, especially Lebanon

(Ingredients):



2 cups finely chopped parsley
30 sheets of green mint or 2 teaspoons mint ground deaf
1/3 cup lemon juice
1/3 cup of bulghur
3 medium tomatoes
5 tablespoons olive oil
3 top green onions, only the white part is used
Salt as desired

2- El moghrabya



with chicken, meat, or both, is a common dish in Lebanon, prepared on most occasions, especially during the blessed month of Ramadan as a basic meal for breakfast or during banquets and lunch invitations among Beirut families. It is a dish that is more known in Lebanese cities than in the countryside. The Moghrabya dish combines delicious taste and complete nutrition. It is prepared from Moghrabya , which is a small spherical dough the size of lentils.

(Ingredients)

.

moghrabya : a cup and a quarter (dry)
- Chicken thighs or meat: 1 kilo (with bone)
- Onions: 16 pieces (small / peeled)
- Chickpeas: 2 cups (boiled and drained)
- Butter: 2 tablespoons
- Vegetable oil: 2 tablespoons
- Water: 7 cups
- Caraway: 2 teaspoons
- Cinnamon: one and a half teaspoon (ground)
- Cumin: a teaspoon (ground)
- Condiment: as desired
- Salt, to taste
